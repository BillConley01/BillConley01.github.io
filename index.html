About

Hello, my name is Bill Conley. Thank you for taking a moment to view my web page. Having always had a passion for technology, my journey towards becoming a software developer began about 10 years ago after I decided to pursue a college education.

Because of my passion for technology the majority of my electives were in programming and other technology related subjects. While going back to school and working fulltime was challenging at first, I became encouraged because I had found a path towards a career that truly excited me.

After graduating I became more clear in my determination to become a software developer and immediately went on to enroll in and complete a master's program in Software Development. To further prepare myself for this new career, I am currently working on a Professional Certificate Program in full stack development.

Although my previous work history has not been in Software Development, I believe the experience, skills and qualities that I have developed and demonstrated throughout my career are desirable and well suited for a software developer. Examples of these skill are a mindset towards continuous improvement and attention to detail, the ability to take on increasing responsibilities, and the ability to work well in a team environment.

These skills paired with my education have uniquely prepared me for the next step in my journey, obtaining a position developing web applications.
